**************************************************
*** Instructions
**************************************************

1. Open SetDesktopIcons.ps1 in Notepad and comment out the shortcuts you don't wish to have on the desktop by adding a #
   at the beginning of the line

2. Add a step to your image creation or deployment task sequence in the State Restore phase to execute the script
